One of the most important ideas that came out of Webstock 2017 was a discussion about ethics in technology.

Our roles in technology give us the ability to affect entire communities and populations. We have, as technologists, an ethical duty to exercise that ability in a responsible manner.

This website then, is designed to offer an overview of the various ethical frameworks that underpin our behaviour as people, while also offering a mutation of the hippocratic oath that might be suitable for our industry to live by.
